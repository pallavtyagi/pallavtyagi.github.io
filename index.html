About

I work at the intersection of Agentic AI and enterprise adoption — helping teams move from experimenting with AI tools to actually depending on them. That means understanding where Claude, ChatGPT, and purpose-built agents genuinely accelerate work, and where they need guardrails before you hand them real access.

A lot of my time goes into building and connecting the pieces: tool integrations, MCP connectors, workflow automations, and the controls that make AI systems auditable and safe enough for regulated environments. I've spent 14+ years in financial services — most recently leading enterprise GenAI adoption and Agentic AI frameworks at Barrenjoey, and before that as a Senior Manager at Macquarie Group. The stakes in regulated industries are high, and that shapes how I think about AI deployment.

Lead engineering for an enterprise Data Lakehouse Platform based on Databricks and open source tech.

I'm also building several micro-SaaS products in the AI space — including SignalWave, a campaign automation platform, and MyGenie in a Bottle, a RAG-based knowledge chatbot platform for organisations.

Outside of that, I share what I'm learning. AI Signals: Daily Dose is my daily podcast covering what's actually moving in AI. When a topic needs more depth than a 10-minute episode allows, I write about it here.
